🌟Plan Your Stay in Viana do Castelo

🏛️ Historic Center Walk

⏱️ 1-2 hours 🚶 Easy walk

Explore the beautiful historic center with its traditional Portuguese architecture, charming squares, and local shops.

⛪ Santa Luzia Sanctuary

⏱️ Half day 🚗 Short drive

Visit the hilltop sanctuary for breathtaking views over Viana do Castelo and the coast. Accessible by car or taxi for those who prefer not to walk uphill.

🏖️ Coastal Activities

⏱️ Half/Full day 🚗 Short drive

Enjoy beautiful beaches, nautical sports, and coastal walks. Perfect for families and outdoor enthusiasts.

🌿 Minho Region

⏱️ Full day 🚗 Day trips

Explore the broader Minho region with its green landscapes, traditional villages, and excellent gastronomy.

🌉 Porto Adventure

⏱️ Full day 🚌 1 hour by bus

Visit Porto, Portugal's second city, famous for its port wine, historic center, and beautiful riverside views.

🍷 Gastronomy

⏱️ Anytime 🍽️ Local cuisine

Experience authentic Portuguese cuisine, fresh seafood, and the famous Vinho Verde wines of the region.

♿Accessibility

Venue Accessibility

The conference venue is 100% wheelchair accessible with flat-level floors, elevator access, and accessible restrooms throughout.

Accommodation Assistance

We can help you identify wheelchair-accessible hotels in the area. Contact us at organisers@euruko.org

Ruby Safari Accessibility

The Ruby Safari takes place entirely in Viana do Castelo's mostly flat historic center. The walking tour stays within the accessible areas of the city center.
